Mission

The company is marching forward with a mission to Formalize School IT Education. This is an approach with which many schools said bye… bye to the non methodical courses and the respective courseware. The company’s ever dynamic syllabus stirs up the students’ brains and tunes them to the updated software versions.

Whom we are?

Munukutla Information Systems [P] Limited was first established on 8th May, 1992 as a small firm with name Institute of Computer Education and was limited to Visakhapatnam District of Andhra Pradesh alone in marketing its prime product school education. Later, when the founder understood that there are many other institutes in the same business line with the same name, changed its name to Institute of Software Education and Applications, a unique name started spreading the product to various other nearby districts – Srikakulam, Vizianagaram, East Godavari etc. From the year 1998, far off places also started recognizing the services of the firm. Schools from Khammam, Warangal and Karimnagar Districts of A.P. too started enrolling themselves as customers to the firm.


Later in 2000, the millennium year, the firm was dissolved and a private limited company – Munukutla Information Systems [P] Limited was incorporated with the founders being the Promotional Directors. For facilitated ‘word of mouth marketing’ the company has been called MINFOS. The first achievement after the firm became the Company was, getting into Orissa state with a CNI Ministries school in 2001. Later in 2004, the company even got into Tamilnadu State, apart from establishing good market segments in other districts like West Godavari, Krishna, Guntur, Ranga Reddy, Medak, Nizamabad, Mahaboobnagar and Nalgonda Districts of Andhra Pradesh. Now the company is rendering its services to many schools in Andhra Pradesh and Tamilnadu.


In 2007, Minfos is approved by a Government of India Organisation, National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC), Hyderabad to conduct some Diploma and Certificate courses throughout the state of Andhra Pradesh for NSIC certification. This approval is on annual basis. The company got it for 2009-2010 academic year too. However, the approval is limited to Andhra Pradesh alone.

What we do?

At Minfos, we are specialists in designing and executing school level computer courses. Since its inception as a firm in early 90s, the firm has been in the same field. Until year 2002, two years after the company was incorporated, it used to take up complete computer contracts in the schools. These contracts used to include even the installation of computers and appointment and maintenance of faculties. But, following the market requirements and the changing trends in the industry as well as the increasing IQ levels of the school-goers, the company came out with a new process – exclusive computer academics maintenance.


This new process is aimed at educating the school-students in computers from class – I to class – X with an innovative fervor. The ever-changing syllabus is a clear evidence for this approach. The company believes that the education cannot be complete unless the students are updated with the latest trends and techniques in the industry from time to time. Our new process reserves a space for this. We also believe that the objective can be achieved only when the computer teachers are also updated with these changing trends of the industry.


This approach is appreciated by many Roman Catholic Institutions throughout Andhra Pradesh as well as some parts of Tamilnadu.

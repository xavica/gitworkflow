Diploma courses

Diploma courses for NSIC (National Small Industries Corporation, A Govt. of India Enter’rise) certification (Vide Approval Letter No. NSIC/TSC/HYD/MinFOS/2009-10, date 16th May, 2009), applicable within Andhra Pradesh only.

Office Software Technician

This course is intended for the students who are studying class – X in a recognized school. The students should have undergone training in Windows and MS Office by the time they attempt to write the Examination. The Courseware-cum-Examination fee would beRs. 425/- per student. This includes the certification charges of NSIC too. The course contents are Windows and MS Office. This is a special discounted programme for school-goers.

Diploma in Computer

This programme is conducted as a non-school programme for the enthusiasts who wish to undergo a training programme for NSIC certification
during non-school hours.
The contents of this course are Windows and MS Office.
The Fee for the course would be Rs.725/- per student,
which includes training, courseware, Examination
and NSIC certification.

Diploma in DTP software

This programme is conducted as a non-school programme for the
enthusiasts who wish to undergo a training programme for NSIC certification during non-school hours. The contents of this course are Windows, PageMaker and Photoshop. The Fee for the course would be Rs.725/- per student, which includes training, courseware,
Examination and NSIC certification.

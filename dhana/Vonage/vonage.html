How Vonage Works

Vonage's VoIP phone service helps you save money every month by using your existing high speed Internet connection to make calls in the US and internationally to more than 60 countries. You can also take your home number with you anywhere you use your smartphone. Make and receive calls via a Wi-Fi or 3G/4G connection (standard data rates apply) and you get the same great rates you get at home - and don't miss important calls while away.
